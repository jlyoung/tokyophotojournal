Welcome

ようこそ

This is the photo-journal of Anthony's trip to Japan in the Spring of 2014. Anthony's good friend Joe lived in Tokyo for 6 years but was soon scheduled to relocate to Europe. Seizing on the opportunity to get in an excursion while Joe was still in town, Anthony bought a flight on the spot, packed his bags and headed for the bright lights of Tokyo.

Shibuya

We'll start this journey in the ward known as Shibuya. Shibuya's one of the premier entertainment districts of Tokyo. You can find everything here from shopping, to dining, to nightclubs. It's central location also serves as a defacto meeting spot for getting together with friends spread throughout the city.
